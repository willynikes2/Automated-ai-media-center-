FAQ

Questions? Answered.

What is CutDaCord?
CutDaCord is a managed media platform that gives you your own personal streaming service. Request any movie or TV show, and it's automatically added to your library and available to stream on all your devices.
What devices can I watch on?
CutDaCord works on iPhone, Android, web browsers, Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, Android TV, Samsung/LG smart TVs, and desktop apps for Windows, Mac, and Linux — powered by the Jellyfin ecosystem.
How fast does content become available?
Most popular movies and shows are available within minutes of your request. New releases depend on availability but are typically fulfilled within hours.
Is my data private?
Yes. Each user gets their own isolated environment. We use encrypted connections, VPN protection for all downloads, and never share your data with third parties.
Can I bring my own IPTV?
Premium users can add their own legal IPTV sources. We merge multiple sources into one clean channel guide with timezone-corrected program listings.
What's the AI Assistant?
Coming soon — a built-in assistant that helps you discover content, manage your library, and control playback with natural language. "What should I watch tonight?" just works.
